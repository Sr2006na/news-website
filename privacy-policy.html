Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2025

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data, including your news preferences, pagination history, and comment history. To exercise these rights, contact us at sreenugirikp123@gmail.com.

Pagination Data

We collect data on your pagination interactions (e.g., pages visited) to improve navigation efficiency. This data is anonymized and not linked to your personal identity unless required for account-specific support.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our news services, pagination features, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with the revised date, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
